About

I am an Assistant Professor (Junior) at Vellore Institute of Technology with over eight years of teaching and research experience in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My expertise includes Deep Learning, Computer Vision and healthcare-focused AI applications. I am passionate about developing intelligent solutions that bridge research and real-world impact.

Research Experience

My research focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Explainable AI and multimodal biomedical data analysis for Parkinson's Disease diagnosis. I have published several SCI-indexed journal articles in internationally reputed journals including Scientific Reports, IEEE Access and PeerJ. My work combines medical image analysis, computer vision and intelligent healthcare systems to develop practical AI-based diagnostic solutions. I actively collaborate with researchers and healthcare professionals to build innovative technologies that support clinical decision making.
