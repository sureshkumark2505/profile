About

I am an Assistant Professor (Junior) at Vellore Institute of Technology with over eight years of experience in teaching and research in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My expertise includes Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and healthcare-focused AI applications. I am passionate about developing innovative solutions that bridge research and real-world impact while mentoring students and contributing to academic excellence.

Research Experience

My research focuses on developing Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning frameworks for the early diagnosis and severity prediction of Parkinson's Disease using multimodal biomedical data. My doctoral research explores handwritten image analysis, multimodal data fusion, and Explainable AI techniques to improve diagnostic accuracy and clinical decision-making.

I have published multiple SCI-indexed research papers in internationally reputed journals including Scientific Reports, IEEE Access, and PeerJ Computer Science. My work primarily contributes to AI-driven healthcare, medical image analysis, computer vision, and intelligent diagnostic systems.

I actively collaborate on interdisciplinary research projects involving healthcare technologies and continue to develop reliable, scalable, and clinically useful AI solutions. My research interests also include Explainable AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Medical Imaging, Pattern Recognition, and Intelligent Healthcare Systems.
